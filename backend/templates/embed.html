Embed Your Campus Chatbot

To add the chatbot widget to your website, copy the following code snippet and paste it just before the closing </body> tag on every page where you want it to appear.

<script>
    window.chatBotConfig = {
        botId: 'campus-assistant-01' /* Optional: Identify your bot */
    };
</script>
<script src="{{ request.url_root }}widget.js" defer></script>

That's it! The chat widget will automatically appear on your site.
